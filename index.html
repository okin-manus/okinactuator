About OKIN Motion Technology

China's Leading Electric Linear Actuator Manufacturer Since 1982

OKIN Motion Technology — part of the DEWERTOKIN Group — is a globally recognized OEM/ODM manufacturer of electric linear actuators, adjustable bed control systems, wireless hand controls, and complete smart furniture drive solutions. Headquartered in Jiaxing, Zhejiang, China, OKIN supplies furniture brands, medical equipment OEMs, and contract manufacturers across Europe, North America, and Asia-Pacific.

Our product range covers everything a furniture OEM needs: from 24V DC linear actuators and multi-motor bed control boxes to smart sleep anti-snore systems, sonic massage sets, RGB under-bed LED lighting, and Bluetooth audio systems — all engineered in-house and certified for global markets. A direct competitor to LINAK HOMELINE, RICHMAT, KAIDI, and Jiecang systems, with the added advantage of one-stop OEM sourcing.

Smart Furniture Ecosystem

Beyond the Actuator — Complete Smart Living Solutions

OKIN supplies the full accessory ecosystem that turns a standard adjustable bed into a connected, voice-controlled, intelligent sleep and wellness platform. All systems integrate with OKIN control boxes, Amazon Alexa, and the SmartBed app.

AI-driven snore detection and intelligent adjustable bed position intervention. Monitors breathing, heart rate, and body movement in real time. Full smart home compatibility — Alexa voice control, Google Home, BLE SmartBed App.

  • Sleep sensor + snore collector (desktop/bedside options)
  • 3 anti-snore intervention intensities: Low / Medium / High
  • Real-time breathing rate, heart rate, body movement monitoring
  • Daily / weekly / monthly sleep reports via iOS & Android app
  • Alexa voice control · SmartBed Wi-Fi app · split-king ready
  • Up to 2 sleep sensors for dual-user monitoring

FP28 Sonic Massage Set

Sound-vibration fusion technology with scientifically designed comfort therapy. The premium upgrade that differentiates your adjustable bed from standard massage motor systems — featuring music synchronization and Bluetooth audio streaming.

  • Up to 4 sonic massage vibrators + 2 loudspeakers
  • 7 massage modes · 3 intensity levels (0-1-2-3)
  • Music massage mode: vibrates in sync with Bluetooth audio
  • Dual 20W stereo loudspeakers · 30Hz–20kHz frequency
  • Remote + SmartBed App control · FCC PART 15C certified

Under-Bed LED Lighting System

Integrated ambient and motion sensor under-bed light for adjustable bed systems. Plug-and-play integration with CB.24.42 and CB.24.22 control boxes — no extra wiring or control box required.

Bluetooth Audio System

Premium in-bed Bluetooth smart home audio delivering full-range stereo. The perfect smart living upgrade for adjustable beds — app control, voice assistant pairing, and independent 29V DC power supply.

Technology & R&D

Built on Precision Engineering.
Proven at Production Scale.

OKIN's in-house R&D team of 300+ engineers drives continuous innovation across electric linear actuator design, smart control electronics, IoT connectivity, and sleep technology — all manufactured at scale from our Jiaxing, Zhejiang production campus. Our output is directly benchmark-tested against LINAK, RICHMAT, KAIDI, and Jiecang specifications.

01
Precision Linear Actuator Engineering

Proprietary gear mechanism design with sub-millimeter positional accuracy across 20–400 mm stroke lengths. Full in-house die-casting, CNC machining and tooling for dimensional consistency at OEM production volume.

02
Smart Control Electronics & IoT Integration

MCU-based control boards with BLE, RF 433 MHz, Wi-Fi module support. Alexa skill integration, SmartBed app (iOS/Android), and custom firmware available for branded OEM app experiences and proprietary handset protocols.

03
100,000-Cycle Safety & Compliance Testing

Dedicated test labs for 100,000-cycle endurance validation, EMC, dielectric strength, anti-pinch force measurement, and certification support — CE, UL, IEC 60601, BIFMA. Full documentation shipped with every product.

04
Full OEM / ODM Customization Capability

Stroke length, gearbox ratio, voltage, connector, colour, button layout, branded PCB and private-label packaging — MOQ-flexible tooling programs from prototype to mass production. Your brand, our engineering.
