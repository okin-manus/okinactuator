About OKIN

Engineering Intelligent
Drive Solutions Since 1982

OKIN Motion Technology (DEWERTOKIN Group) is a globally recognized manufacturer of smart linear actuators, control electronics, and complete drive systems for the furniture, medical, and office ergonomics industries.

From our advanced R&D centers and manufacturing base in Guangdong, China, we deliver OEM-grade components and complete integrated systems to furniture brands and contract manufacturers across Europe, North America, and Asia-Pacific.

Technology & R&D

Built on Precision.
Proven by Performance.

OKIN's in-house R&D team of 300+ engineers drives continuous innovation — from custom gear train design to IoT-integrated control electronics.

01
Precision Drive Engineering

Proprietary gear mechanism design with sub-millimeter positional accuracy. Full in-house tooling and die-casting for consistent dimensional control at production volume.

02
Smart Control Electronics

MCU-based control boards with BLE / RF / CAN bus support. Custom firmware development available for OEM partners requiring proprietary handset protocols or app integration.

03
Safety & Compliance Testing

Dedicated test labs for 100,000-cycle endurance validation, EMC, dielectric strength, anti-pinch force measurement, and certification test support (CE, UL, IEC).

04
OEM Customization Capability

Stroke length, gearbox ratio, color, connector, and branded PCB — OKIN supports full OEM customization with MOQ-flexible tooling programs from prototype to mass production.
